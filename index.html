About Me

Composer | Producer | Sound Designer

I graduated with a B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering from Vels University. While studying, I developed a strong passion for music and began my journey in FL Studio before moving to Ableton Live. Since then, I've worked on short films, pilot projects, albums, and songs, continuing to grow as a composer and music producer.

I work on composition, music production, sound design, arranging, and mixing, and I'm always experimenting with new sounds and techniques
